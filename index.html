The Problem

After doing this for the last 10 years, I've learnt how to navigate those problems and create systems and frameworks to create consistent content that people actually want to watch.

My Story

I Started Making Videos In 2011. I've Never Stopped.

2011

Started making videos after school at the skate park with friends.

2016

Started taking Instagram seriously and became obsessed with understanding why people watch content.

2018

Dropped out of university. I was failing and didn't like the path I was on. Studied at Media Design School and got a Bachelor of Media Design, 2019 to 2021.

2019–2021

Working construction. Trying to find my foot in the industry. A man named Stanley Henry gave me that chance when I joined The Attention Seeker, when it was just a LinkedIn ghostwriting thing. Little did I know it would go on to become the biggest social media marketing agency in the country.

2022

Joined as their first videographer. I'd failed engineering, was working construction. Stanley took a chance on me.

2022–2025

During that time I:

  • Helped scale the team from 6 to 40 people
  • Built the production arm from 1 to 14 staff
  • Worked with 200+ brands
  • Built the highest engaged ad globally on TikTok with One NZ
  • Toured with Six60 creating social content
  • Generated millions of views across campaigns
  • Grew my own channels to 100,000+ followers
2023

Started Daplaza, my clothing brand. Made a video every single day for 330+ days.

2024

Started my personal brand. I lost the passion to keep Daplaza going, and my attention switched. I understood the long-term value of a personal brand. If I had attention and a brand, I could get to a point where I could do whatever I want.

2025

Signed with No Sad Cowboys talent agency. Now doing UGC for brands like Pump, Woolworths, Anchor, Extra Gum, La Roche-Posay, Garnier, Chemist Warehouse, Musashi.

2026+

I reached a point where things felt comfortable, and that's exactly when I knew I needed to take a risk. Now I'm chasing my own dream.

"Everything I do for other people I've done for myself."
